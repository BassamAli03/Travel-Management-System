FAQ's

Tour Management System

Q: What is our system about?

A: Tour Management System is a multi component system where a customer can book their flights, book a room and rent a car at the exact same time. They can choose from the available options of each and pay online.

Q: What is the refund policy?

A: We offer a refund policy on cancellation time of maximum 24 hours. If you try to cancel after that, you will not be given any refund amount.

Q: Is my information safe?

A: The customer's information is highly encrypted and stored in our servers where it is secure. We also have a working local storage to temporarily access the customer's data and then clear it when the work is done.

Q: Are my transactions secure?

A: All the payment details of the customer are encrypted and verified through a secure source. The confirmation is sent to the customer directly.
